## Why Astro?

Astro is a content-first static site framework — perfect for blogs and portfolio sites.

### Highlights

- **Island Architecture** — JavaScript only loads where interactivity is needed
- **Content Collections** — built-in Markdown content management
- **Multi-framework support** — mix and match React, Vue, or Svelte

### The Build

The whole site went from zero to deployed in roughly an afternoon. Highly recommended!
